Mark Kampe

Mark Kampe has been working on operating systems (as a developer, an architect, a manager, and in staff roles) since 1972. He has worked extensively in platform support, file systems, multi-processor support, distributed systems, high availability, management tools, and development methodology for both start-ups and enterprise software companies.

He is currently the VP of Engineering for a distributed storage start-up: Inktank.

He likes to share his experience by teaching courses Operating Systems and Software Engineering.
