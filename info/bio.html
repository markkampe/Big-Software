Mark Kampe

Mark Kampe has retired from a career in operating systems, working as a developer, an architect, a manager, and in staff roles since 1972. He has worked extensively in OS ports, file systems, multi-processor support, distributed systems, high availability, high performance and development methodology for both start-ups and enterprise software companies.

He likes to share his experience by teaching courses in Operating Systems and Software Engineering, acting as a Clinic advisor, and greatly enjoys playing in the lab with intro programming students.
