With appologies to Sun Tzu:

    "The art of software development is of vital importance to every enterprise.
    It is a matter of life and death, a road either to success or to ruin.
    Hence it is a subject of inquiry which can on no account be neglected."

The goal of this course is to familiarize you with the concepts, tools and techniques that are required to successfully pursue projects ranging from a few staff-months to a staff-century or more. It is not a survey course. It is a very practical course dealing with real-world problems, the issues that complicate them, and the approaches that have been successfully applied to their solution.

The course content is described in the summary of learning objectives.

To simplify/eliminate note taking, all of the lecture slides are available on-line ... although they are subject to change up to lecture time.
