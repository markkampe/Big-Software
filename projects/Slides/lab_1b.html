Competitive Research
(due Sunday night)
  • P1B Competitive Research
    • What similar products have been built and deployed?
      and which of those have the most to teach you?
    • What can they tell you about features?
    • What can they tell you about the user communities?
    • What can they tell you about deployment approaches?
    • What can they tell you about implementation technogies?
  • P1B Competitive Positioning
    • How can you improve on their targeting, features or delivery?
  • P1C Moving Forward
    • What things, that they did, do you want to use?
    • What mistakes, that they made, do you want to avoid?
    • How does this change your thoughts about what you want to build?
