Selected Readings in Software Engineering

Other than one excellent primary text on software development (McConnel's Code Complete), I have attempted to avoid forcing you to buy a Software Engineering text. I found that I could cover much of the syllabus with classic papers, columns from respected people, and Wikipedia articles. But there were still several areas where I could not find adequate introductory papers.

To fill those needs I wrote these papers (or in a few cases, adapted papers I had written during my career as an engineer). Thus, the following collection cannot pretend to be a Software Engineering Textbook. These are merely a collection of monographs to fill in areas where I felt the coverage from other freely available sources to be inadequate.
